📖 Sight Word Reader Tool

Build sight-word fluency together — one story at a time!

📖 How to Use

This tool helps your child build sight-word fluency by reading stories where key words are hidden. Tap the masked words to reveal them!

1. Sight Words

  • Create word sets to organize sight words by level or theme.
  • Type words into the input box and press Add. You can add several at once separated by spaces.
  • Click the × on any word tag to remove it.

2. Stories

  • Add a title, paste or type the story text, and optionally add an illustration.
  • Check the word sets that apply to each story so the reader knows which words to mask.
  • Edit or delete stories any time from the list.

3. Read Together

  • Pick a story from the dropdown to start reading.
  • 🔒 Mask All hides every sight word. 🔓 Unmask All reveals them.
  • Tap any yellow masked word to reveal it — it turns green when shown.
  • The word panels below the story show which sight words appear (and which don't).

Good to Know

  • All data is saved automatically in your browser — no account needed.
  • Works offline once loaded.
